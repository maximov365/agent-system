# Discovery Mode: Brand & Identity

Use this mode when the question is about **brand positioning, naming, tone of voice, or visual identity** for the product.

This mode feeds into the Designer agent and Product agent — its output informs `docs/BRAND.md`.

---

## Additional responsibilities

- Research brand positioning of 3–5 competitors or aspirational references
- Analyze naming conventions in the target market
- Identify tone-of-voice patterns (formal, casual, technical, playful)
- Research visual identity trends in the product category
- Consider cultural and linguistic implications of brand choices
- Evaluate trademark/domain availability when relevant (use web search)

---
