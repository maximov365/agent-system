# Discovery Mode: Marketing & Go-to-Market

Use this mode when the question is about **go-to-market strategy, channels, messaging, pricing, or distribution** for the product.

This mode feeds into the Product agent — its output informs feature prioritization and launch strategy.

---

## Additional responsibilities

- Research distribution channels used by 3–5 competitors
- Analyze pricing models in the target market
- Identify acquisition channels (organic, paid, partnerships, communities)
- Research messaging strategies and value propositions competitors use
- Evaluate community and content marketing approaches
- Consider platform-specific distribution (App Store, web, enterprise sales)
- Identify early adopter segments and launch strategies

---
